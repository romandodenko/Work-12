About us

Welcome to the Sheraton Grand Sydney Hyde Park Pokies Club— the perfect place for a comfortable stay and an unforgettable vacation! Located in the heart of Sydney, our hotel offers a high level of service and luxurious amenities to make your stay perfect. We strive to be the best, so we offer spacious and comfortable rooms, panoramic views of the city and a great gaming atmosphere. We are proud of our high standards of service and strive to make your stay unforgettable.

Conveniences

Our rooms are equipped with love of detail and will provide you with maximum comfort. In each room you will find everything modern, including air conditioning, a flat-screen TV, a minibar and free Wi-Fi. In addition, our hotel offers guests a fitness center, a spa and a swimming pool for complete relaxation and wellness. And if you are staying for a business trip, we are ready to provide conference rooms, special equipment and meeting rooms. We also want to thank you for choosing our hotel by offering you a special 10% discount on your reservation.
